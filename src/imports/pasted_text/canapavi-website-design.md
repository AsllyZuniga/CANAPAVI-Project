ORGANIZATION INFORMATION
==================================================

Organization:
Fundación Cultura Nariñense para el Rescate de los Valores e Identidad (CANAPAVI)

Location:
Tumaco, Nariño, Colombia

Address:
Avenida La Playa con Calle Páez 3-42

Phone:
316 415 7472

Mission:
Protect, preserve, strengthen and promote the cultural identity, ancestral knowledge, intangible heritage and collective memory of the Colombian South Pacific, especially Afro-Colombian communities, through education, culture, art, territorial defense, community participation and women's leadership.

Vision:
Become a national and international reference in the preservation of Afro-Pacific cultural heritage, strengthening communities through social innovation, education, artivism, human rights and sustainable territorial development.

Main work areas:
- Afro-Colombian cultural heritage
- Human rights
- Women's empowerment
- Territorial defense
- Community participation
- Intergenerational dialogue
- Artivism
- Ethnoeducation
- Traditional music
- Cantadoras
- Oral traditions
- Sound weaving ("Tejidos Sonoros")
- Community leadership
- Youth participation
- Environmental protection
- Sustainable development

The organization collaborates with national and international institutions including ONU Mujeres and other organizations supporting women defenders and territorial rights.
